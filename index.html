How PropGuard Works

The system is designed for funded traders who need structure before and after each trade.

1

Configure your account rules

Set your prop firm, drawdown limits, daily loss limit, starting balance, and max risk per trade.

2

Calculate risk before execution

PropGuard tells you size, risk per contract, daily impact, drawdown impact, and whether the trade is safe.

3

Track behavior after each trade

Log wins, losses, or break-even trades and instantly update consistency, survival, session tracking, and equity curve.
